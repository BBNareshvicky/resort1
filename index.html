Adult Only All-Inclusive Resort

An island oasis for downtime and disconnecting, Forbidden Resort rewrites the rule book on inclusive getaways. Our intimate adults-only (ages 21+ only) resort sits within a botanical garden edged by 1000 feet of ocean shoreline, just a few miles from the warm waters of the Gulf Stream. We take care of all the details, because the time you'll spend together is the greatest luxury of all.

Kayaking

Kayaking is a fun water sport. It involves
the use of a kayak, a narrowboat with a
double-blade paddle to move on water.
The driver is supposed to navigate
through the waterways by sitting face-
forward and marching ahead with alternating
side-to-side paddle strokes. Kayaking ways
originally invented for hunting.

White-Water-Rafting

Rafting is a sport in which rafters
use an inflatable raft to race with
other teams. The team that reaches
the destination first wins the race.
The racing is done on white water or
rough water of different degrees. The
rafters use double blades to the boat.
